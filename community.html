Charlotte Mecklenburg Schools

This was the final presentation for the development and implementation of Express Stops. This project was initially catalyzed when the numerous parent, student, and administrator complaints were registered. Inequalities in transportation services across the school district were highlighted and advocated for, necessitating a solution.
Subsequently, express stops were developed— a solution that grouped kids in nearby stops to one stop. This allowed for thousands of hours of saved time for students and drivers and saved over 1.3 million miles of driving and millions in maintenance on buses.

T-Care Project with Levine's Children

After my sickness for most of freshman year, I saw the disparity in donations. Specifically for teenagers in long-term care, there are often gaps in what goods are donated that are age-specific. As such, I have developed a project to work with large Charlotte children's hospitals to help teenage patients in long-term care.
Using Python, JavaScript, SQL, and other web-dev languages (CSS/HTML), I created a portal for teenagers to request specific things they may want. Below, you can see images of that portal. Once I get approval from Levine's Children/Atrium for implementation, we hope to do a trial run as soon as possible.
Using the hospital name field, the program uses Google API for reverse geocoding to find and input the address of the hospital as well. While we work exclusively at one hospital this will not be necessary, however, I hope to expand this project to other care facilities as well.

Lions Club Ahmedabad - Diabetes Awareness

Photos and description of my work with Lions Club Ahmedabad in India for diabetes awareness:

After watching my grandmother struggle through her battle with diabetes, as I watched her health deteriorate I realized the true extent of the damage diabetes does. Working with a contact in Lions Club Naroda, we organized 3 camps that saw 500+ senior citizens in total.
We discussed diabetes, prevention measures, what to do after diagnosis, and helped spread knowledge about how to test insulin levels.

Please visit the Lions Club International — Naroda Website to learn more about their cause.
